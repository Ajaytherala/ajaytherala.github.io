About Me

A dedicated Computer Science Graduate Student at Arizona State University, maintaining a 4.0 GPA. I have earned my Bachelor's Degree from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) with a C.G.P.A of 9.21, majoring in Information Technology.

During my penultimate year, I interned at TCS Hyderabad on a research project focused on data refinement and machine learning algorithms for network classification & malware detection. Working with datasets like NetML, CICIDS2017, and non-vpn2016, I explored various techniques and achieved significant results, including placing in the top 5 of the NetML - Network Traffic Analytics Challenge 2020. I have presented my findings at the ICAIIC 2022 Conference, showcasing my ability to share insights and collaborate with industry professionals.

Following my internship, I transitioned into a full-time ML Developer role at TCS. In this position, I played a key role in developing components for TCS Cognitive Product Support, an intelligent domain-specific search engine. Notably, I developed the Data Lens component for training custom NER models and the Brickwork component for annotating entities and relationships from PDF documents. My work included significant contributions to GPT-powered bots, demonstrating my expertise in Generative AI, GPT Embeddings, and Prompt Engineering. I have also gained hands-on experience in web development and proficiency with AWS services such as AWS S3, Textract by delivering presentations on Pocs using the AWS.

My technical skills encompass C, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3.js, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Git, Django, Flask, Azure ML Studio, AWS, Elastic Search, Generative AI, Knowledge Graphs, SQL Server, MongoDB, HDFS, and Apache Spark. I have earned certifications as a Microsoft Certified Azure Data Scientist and an Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Generative AI Certified Professional.

My projects involve advanced technologies such as Generative AI, Deep Learning, and Visualization techniques, with specific work on analyzing hallucinations in MLLMs and text similarity using Siamese Networks.

I am highly motivated and thrive in environments that value innovation and organizational success. I am eager to bring my skills and experience to your company and contribute to your continued success.
